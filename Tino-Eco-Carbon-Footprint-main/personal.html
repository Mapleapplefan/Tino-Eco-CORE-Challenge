32. Personal action

Planting native seeds (40 pts)

Research your native ecosystem and pick three plants that you are interested in. Why are these three native plants important (what role do they play in the ecosystem)? Are they endangered? Choose one of the native plants and plant it in a pot or garden. (300 words minimum)

If you want, use an old egg carton to create your own planters!

33. Personal action

Starting a composting bin (40 pts, extra 10 every additional 2 week)

Starting a composting bin

Create a plan for your compost bin "project". Think about the type of compost bin, what types of food to use, wildlife concerns. *Research HIGHLY recommended

Learn about Aerobic vs Anaerobic composting (2 similarities/2 differences)

Maintain the compost bin for a week

Reflect on how the week went or any takeaways.

Take pictures of the process and results, send them to us
